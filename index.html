Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you make informed decisions

What services does Royal Rajasthan Interiors offer? +
We offer a comprehensive range of interior services including Modular Kitchen design, complete Interior Designing, custom Furniture manufacturing, Almari/Doors/Windows solutions, and TV Unit Interiors. Each service is tailored to your unique requirements and budget.
How long does a typical interior project take? +
The timeline varies based on project scope. A single room makeover typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a full home interior project can take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation and ensure timely delivery.
Do you offer free design consultations? +
Yes! We offer a completely free initial design consultation. Our experts will visit your site, understand your requirements, take measurements, and provide preliminary design ideas along with a detailed cost estimate — all at no charge.
What materials do you use for your projects? +
We use only premium-quality, durable materials including marine plywood, HDHMR boards, Italian laminates, soft-close hardware, and eco-friendly finishes. All materials come with warranty and are sourced from trusted brands for lasting quality.
Do you provide warranty on your work? +
Absolutely! We provide a comprehensive warranty on all our interior work. The warranty period varies by product — typically 5-10 years on modular furniture and kitchen installations. We also offer after-sales maintenance support.
What areas do you serve? +
While we are based in Jaipur, we serve clients across Rajasthan including Jodhpur, Udaipur, Ajmer, Kota, and surrounding areas. For projects outside Jaipur, we have a seamless remote consultation process with on-site visits as needed.
